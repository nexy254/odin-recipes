This is a recipe for zeppole!

This page will show you how to make a batch of zeppole!!

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts vegetable oil!
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour!
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder!
  • 1 ½ teaspoon of white sugar!
  • 1 pinch of salt!
  • 2 large eggs, beaten!
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese!
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract!
  • ½ cup confectioners' sugar for dusting!
  1. Heat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees F!
  2. Combine flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a medium saucepan. Stir in eggs, cheese and vanilla, then mix over low heat!
  3. Drop tablespoons of batter into oil and fry until golden brown!
