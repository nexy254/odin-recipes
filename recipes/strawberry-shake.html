Strawberry Shake

This is a recipe for a refreshing strawberry shake!

This page will show you how to make a simple strawberry shake! This one is very easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk!
  • 1 cup of frozen strawberries!
  • 1 tablespoon of honey!
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract!

Steps

  1. Combine your milk, frozen strawberries, honey and vanilla into a blender!
  2. Blend until smooth!
  3. Serve!
