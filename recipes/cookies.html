Cookies

This is a recipe for delectable chocolate chip cookies!

This page will show you how to make a batch of delicious chocolate chip cookies using the ingredients shown!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Beat your butter and sugars, then beat your eggs and vanilla!
  2. Dissolve your baking soda in hot water and add it into the mix!
  3. Stir in your flour and chocolate chips!
  4. Place your dough onto a prepared baking sheet!
  5. Bake at 350 degrees F for 10 minutes or until the edges are golden brown!
